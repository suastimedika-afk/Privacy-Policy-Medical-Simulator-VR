Privacy Policy – Medical Simulator VR

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

Welcome to Medical Simulator VR. Your privacy and the integrity of your professional training data are of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Username or ID used to save training progress and certification records.
  • Simulation Performance Data: Scores, response times, procedural accuracy, and module completion history.
  • Technical Data: VR hardware specifications and crash logs for stability and performance optimization.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Providing feedback on clinical performance
  • Improving medical simulation accuracy
  • Internal research for healthcare education improvement

3. Content and Age Restrictions (18+)

This application is designed for healthcare professionals and medical students only. Users must be at least 18 years of age.

The simulation contains realistic anatomical representations and childbirth procedures intended strictly for medical education and professional training.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical security measures to protect user data. We do not sell or share user data with third parties.

5. Third-Party Platforms

Distribution platforms such as Meta Quest Store or SteamVR may collect their own data according to their respective privacy policies.

6. User Consent

By using Medical Simulator VR, you consent to this Privacy Policy and understand that this simulation is an educational tool and not a substitute for professional medical judgment.

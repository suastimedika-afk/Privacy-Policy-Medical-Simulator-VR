Privacy Policy – Medical Simulator VR

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

Welcome to Medical Simulator VR. Your privacy and the integrity of your professional training data are of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information within our medical simulation application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect data strictly necessary to support the medical learning experience, including:

  • Account Data: Username or ID used to save training progress and certification records.
  • Simulation Performance Data: Scores, response times, procedural accuracy, and module completion history for educational evaluation.
  • Technical Data: VR hardware specifications and crash logs to ensure application stability and performance optimization.

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used solely for:

  • Providing feedback on the user's clinical performance.
  • Improving the accuracy of medical simulations.
  • Internal research purposes to enhance healthcare training curricula.

3. Content and Age Restrictions (18+)

This application is specifically designed for healthcare professionals, medical/midwifery students, and academic institutions.

  • Age Requirement: Users must be at least 18 years of age or the age of legal majority in their jurisdiction.
  • Explicit Medical Content: This simulation contains realistic anatomical representations and childbirth procedures. By using this application, you acknowledge that you are using this tool for medical education or professional training purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement technical security measures to protect your medical performance data from unauthorized access. We do not sell user data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Platforms

If this application is distributed via platforms such as Meta Quest Store or SteamVR, please be aware that these platforms may have their own privacy policies regarding hardware data and store transactions.

6. User Consent

By using Medical Simulator VR, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. You understand that this simulation is an educational aid and is not a substitute for professional medical judgment.
